Payment Confirmation

The online mode is the preferred mode of payment using Debit Card/ Credit Card/ RuPay Debit Card/ RuPay Credit Card/ Bhim UPI QR/ UPI/ Wallets or Net Banking. In the online mode, the application form can be submitted and printed instantly.
OR
Candidates can also pay through offline mode by depositing cash in the bank branch. Use cash mode by depositing cash in following banks (Using this mode, challan will be generated which you are required to take to the bank):
1. Indian Bank
2. ICICI Bank
3. Bank of India
4. Airtel Payments Bank
OR
For depositing fee through cash in other banks, you can use NEFT payment option where a slip (NEFT Advice For Payer) will be generated which is required to be taken to the bank.
